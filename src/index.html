Introduction

The integration of environmental, social and governance (ESG) factors into investment strategy is becoming mainstream.

Major players develop their own ESG interpretation and methodology.

With so many opinions, Investors are lost in ESG translation, they are looking for clarity and simplicity.

About Conser

We are passionate people determined to enable a real integration of sustainability into finance. Our commitment is to advice our clients in a neutral and Independent manner. We act as third-party verifier and build trust between investors and asset mangers.

Our mission is to translate the ESG complexity into an accessible journey and to help investors to navigate the responsible investment landscape.
