Founded in 2007, one of the first advisory firm 100% dedicated to sustainable investment.

Conser has played a leading role in the development of the sustainable investment industry in Switzerland, co-founding the two Swiss Finance Sustainability platforms (SFG & SSF) whose goal is to spread responsibility in finance. The company is also Bcop certified.

Since 2018, Conser has made the move to digitalization of its core business.

Conser has successfully worked with over 80 institutional clients - pension funds, foundations and asset managers.
